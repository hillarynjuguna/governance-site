# CIR Primitives

Operationalizing governance requires precise, executable primitives.

- **BVL (Boundary Verification Layer)**: The structural limit of the model's operational mandate.
- **ECC (Epistemic Confidence Constraint)**: How the system measures its own uncertainty.
- **CDLR (Constitutional Default & Liability Routing)**: Fallback mechanisms when bounds are breached.
- **DO (Diagnostic Orchestration)**: The monitoring and telemetry of the governance states.
