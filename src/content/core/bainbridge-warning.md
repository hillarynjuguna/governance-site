# The Bainbridge Warning

*This is the executive field manual for identifying the gap between high AI capability and inadequate governance infrastructure.*

## 1. Governance Theater vs. Behavioral Layer Exposure
Most organizations operate with "Governance Theater" (policy statements). We measure "Behavioral Layer Exposure" (actual operational changes).

## 2. The Warning
The Bainbridge Warning identifies the phenomenon where increased transparency masks decreased reliability or increased restrictions. It is the leading indicator of systemic governance failure.
