# Governed Intelligence

Governed Intelligence is defined by five structural conditions:

1. **CES (Constitutional Enforcement State)**
2. **PBP (Provenance & Boundary Preservation)**
3. **IDL (Intent-to-Deployment Lock)**
4. **SAI (Sovereign Authority Isolation)**
5. **t-Lock (Temporal Governance Lock)**

## Positioning
Unlike NIST or the EU AI Act which prescribe *what* to report, Governed Intelligence prescribes *how* the system must architecturally constrain itself.
